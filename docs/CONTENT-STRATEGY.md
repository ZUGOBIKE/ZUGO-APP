## AI Search Optimization Tactics

### How to show up in ChatGPT / Perplexity / Google AI

1. **Structured Data is King**
   - FAQPage schema on every page with questions
   - Product schema on comparison pages
   - HowTo schema on tutorial pages
   - VideoObject schema when videos are embedded
   - Organization schema with founding details

2. **Write in Q&A Format**
   - AI models pull from content that directly answers questions
   - Every blog should start with a clear answer, then expand
   - Use H2s that are actual questions people ask

3. **Be the Cited Source**
   - Publish original data (customer surveys, range tests, cost comparisons)
   - Create definitive comparison tables AI can reference
   - Be specific: "The ZUGO Rhino's 48V 20Ah battery delivers up to 80 miles" not "great battery life"

4. **Get Mentioned Elsewhere**
   - Reddit r/ebikes — participate genuinely, mention ZUGO where relevant
   - YouTube reviews — send bikes to reviewers
   - Cycling forums and Facebook groups
   - Get listed on "best ebike" roundup articles
   - Respond to HARO/Qwoted journalist queries about e-bikes

5. **Technical SEO**
   - Fast page loads (static site helps)
   - Mobile-first design
   - Internal linking between all content
   - Sitemap.xml with all blog URLs
   - Descriptive alt text on every image

## Video Content Strategy

### Equipment Needed
- Smartphone with good camera (iPhone 13+ or similar)
- $30 clip-on mic (Rode Wireless GO or similar)
- Natural outdoor lighting (Austin has plenty)
- Simple tripod or phone mount

### Video Types
| Type | Length | Platform | Frequency |
|------|--------|----------|-----------|
| Full tutorials | 5-15 min | YouTube | 2/month |
| Quick tips | 30-60 sec | TikTok, Reels, Shorts | 2/week |
| Customer stories | 2-5 min | YouTube + website | 1/month |
| Product demos | 3-8 min | YouTube + website | As needed |

- Title format: "How to [Action] on Your E-Bike | ZUGO Bike"
- First 2 lines of description: keyword-rich summary
- Tags: brand name + topic keywords
