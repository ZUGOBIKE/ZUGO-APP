Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ZUGO e-bikes

What makes ZUGO different from other e-bike brands? +
ZUGO bikes are designed and engineered in Austin, TX with premium components. Every Rhino features a 750W motor, up to 80 miles of range, a 1200+ lumen headlight, and 4-inch fat tires — all starting at $1,999, which undercuts competitors by hundreds of dollars.
How far can a ZUGO bike go on a single charge? +
The ZUGO Rhino can travel up to 80 miles on a single charge in pedal-assist mode. Range varies based on terrain, rider weight, assist level, and weather conditions. The 48V 20Ah battery is one of the largest in its class.
Can I ride with a passenger? +
Yes! The ZUGO Rhino is built for two. With its powerful 750W motor, heavy-duty frame, and oversized rear rack, it's designed to comfortably carry a passenger for commuting, trail riding, or just cruising around town.
Do you offer financing? +
Yes! We've partnered with Sezzle to offer interest-free payment plans. Split your purchase into easy installments and start riding today. Free shipping is included on all e-bike orders.
What's included with every ZUGO bike? +
Every ZUGO Rhino comes fully loaded with a 1200+ lumen headlight, front and rear fenders, a heavy-duty rear rack, 4-inch fat tires, and free shipping. We also plant 10 trees for every bike sold through our reforestation program.
How do I maintain my ZUGO e-bike? +
Basic maintenance includes keeping tires inflated to the recommended PSI, lubricating the chain every 100-200 miles, checking brake pad wear, and storing the battery indoors at room temperature when not in use. Check out our video guides above for step-by-step tutorials.

About ZUGO Bike

ZUGO Bike is an electric bicycle company based in Austin, Texas that designs and engineers premium fat tire e-bikes. The flagship ZUGO Rhino is available in two frame styles — Step-Over and Step-Thru — and is built around a powerful 750-watt rear hub motor paired with a 48V 20Ah lithium battery capable of delivering up to 80 miles of range on a single charge.

Every ZUGO Rhino includes a 1200+ lumen integrated headlight, front and rear fenders, a heavy-duty rear rack rated for passenger use, and 4-inch puncture-resistant fat tires suited for city streets, gravel, trails, and sand. Prices start at $1,999 with free shipping to the continental United States, and interest-free financing is available through Sezzle.

ZUGO is committed to sustainability and plants 10 trees for every bike sold through a reforestation partnership. The company serves commuters, recreational riders, couples, and adventure seekers looking for a reliable, high-value electric bike backed by dedicated customer support and a growing library of free maintenance resources.
